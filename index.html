Views on the industry I'm in

I think a lot of enterprise software suffers from symptoms of over-engineering and a lack of long-sightedness in what tools will likely be in use 10 years from now. That being said, for a lot of software I am working on, re-architecting or rebuilding things "to remake it right" is not a viable option. WHich means that I'm occasionally building things with technoligies I wouldn't really recommend. Building stuff and shipping software that actually make a difference is what's most important (and what makes work gratifying).
